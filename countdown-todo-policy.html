Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-10

Data Usage Summary

Countdown + To-Do is designed to work offline and does not collect, share, or store any personal data on external servers. The only data used by third parties comes from Google services (AdMob and Google Play) to display ads and process purchases. These services may collect device identifiers as described in their own privacy policies.

Local Storage

All your countdowns, events, and tasks are stored locally on your device. You can delete them at any time by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.

Advertising

The free version displays ads. Ad networks such as Google AdMob may collect device identifiers to provide and measure ads. You can manage ad consent within the app settings via Google’s consent dialog.

Pro Upgrade

Purchases are handled securely by Google Play. We do not have access to or store your payment or account information.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or request personal information from anyone under 13 years of age.
